About

Hey there! Welcome to my little creative hub! I'm Kira, an aspiring artist and animator fueled by a love for the unconventional.

In a world where cartoons used to take daring risks, I strive to recapture that spirit in every piece. Forget normal—I'm all about diving into the exciting world of unconventional ideas.

I find inspiration in analyzing art, adding a bit of fuel to my imagination, and admiring the cool expressions of fellow artists.

Art, to me, is an extension of the soul, but animation? It's a whole other level—a meticulous optical illusion crafted from the essence of countless hard working creative souls. Line art, coloring, storyboarding, shading, keyframing, or inbetweening, every task is vital, and I appreciate the value in each one.

I may be a newcomer, but I'm eager to showcase the depth of my creativity. Take a moment to explore my evolving portfolio, where each piece is a testament to my commitment to this amazing medium.

Join me in celebrating the beauty of taking risks and pushing the boundaries of artistic expression. Your visit means the world, and I hope you enjoy the journey with me!"
